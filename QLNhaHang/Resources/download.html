    A Culinary Tour of the Tropics

    Many Restaurants at Tropical Islands

    Embark on a culinary voyage at Tropical Islands' five restaurants. At the Palm Beach Restaurant with its fine view of the Tropical Sea, you can choose from a selection of fish and meat dishes. If you fancy something asian the Asia Buffet is a good choice,  at the Sawadee restaurant offers a variety of tasty dishes. At the Mondial Food Court you can pick up salads, wraps, burgers and pizza inspired by international street food. The Tropical Garden restaurant offers multi-course menus for discerning palates, while children can enjoy typical kids' favourites at the Tropino Restaurant – Tropical Islands caters for all tastes!

    After selecting your tasty lunch, snack or drink, take your food to the central Pasar Food Market and enjoy it in the company of your friends.
